About UniCalc

UniCalc is a free, comprehensive platform designed to help Pakistani students calculate their university admission merit accurately. We support 15+ top universities and provide precise aggregate calculations for both FSc and A-Level students.

Our Mission

To empower Pakistani students with accurate, free tools to plan their academic future and make informed decisions about university admissions.

Accuracy

We use official merit formulas from each university and update them annually to ensure you get the most accurate calculations possible.

Community

Join thousands of students who have used UniCalc to successfully plan their university admissions and achieve their academic goals.
