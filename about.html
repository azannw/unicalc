About UniCalc

This is by a Pakistani, for Pakistanis. UniCalc is a small effort from my side for my beloved younger Pakistani students who are working hard to break into the top universities of this country. I genuinely want to make your lives a little easier during what is probably one of the most stressful times you will go through.

Every university has its own merit formula. Some weight Matric at 10%, others at 40%. A-Level conversions differ across universities too. And if you try to Google the formula, you will get five different answers from five different sources. UniCalc fixes that. One calculator, every major university, the correct official formula. Enter your marks, pick your university, get your aggregate. That is it.

We cover 17+ universities including NUST, FAST, GIKI, ITU, COMSATS, UET, NED, PIEAS, and more. Both FSc and A-Level systems are fully supported.

I really want more and more people in Pakistan to break into STEM out of pure interest and curiosity. I would love to see STEM education growing in our country, and if this tool helps even one student figure out where they stand and aim higher, it was worth building.

UniCalc is Pakistan's first open-source university merit calculator. The entire codebase is public on GitHub. Anyone can inspect it, contribute, or suggest improvements. This app is completely free. No ads, no hidden fees, no signups. Just come, check your merit, and go.

Huge thanks to the contributors who helped me launch this. Without them it would have been very hard to bring this level of quality and accuracy for you guys. Formulas are updated every admission cycle, and merit data is verified and cross-checked by people who have actually been through the process.

If you love this tool, you can remember me and the contributors in your prayers :)

You can also join our entry test community where seniors are present to help. You can discuss, get guidance, mentorship, and ask your queries. Join us on WhatsApp or Discord.
